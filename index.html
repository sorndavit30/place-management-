Welcome to Cambodia's Travel Destination

Siem Reap is a charming city in northwestern Cambodia, best known as the gateway to the iconic Angkor Wat temple complex. It is a destination that combines ancient history, vibrant culture, and modern comforts, making it a favorite spot for travelers from around the world. At the heart of Siem Reap's appeal is Angkor Archaeological Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visitors can explore awe-inspiring temples like Angkor Wat, Bayon, and Ta Prohm, famous for its giant trees intertwined with ancient stone ruins. These breathtaking landmarks offer a glimpse into the grandeur of the Khmer Empire. Beyond the temples, Siem Reap has plenty to offer. The Old Market and Angkor Night Market are great places to shop for unique souvenirs, handmade crafts, and delicious street food. The lively Pub Street is perfect for enjoying local and international cuisine or experiencing the city's nightlife. For a cultural experience, the Phare Circus showcases impressive acrobatics, music, and storytelling. Visitors can also take a relaxing boat ride on Tonle Sap Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Southeast Asia, to witness floating villages and learn about the local way of life. Siem Reap also caters to modern travelers with its luxurious hotels, cozy guesthouses, and spa retreats. Whether you're an adventurer, history lover, or simply looking to relax, Siem Reap offers an unforgettable experience that blends the past and present in a truly magical way.

Koh Ker is a remote archeological site in the north of Preah Vihear province of Cambodia, about 120 km from Siem Reap and the site of Angkor. It is an area full of many forests with sparsely populated areas. More than 180 shelters have been found in the 81-square-kilometer protected area.
